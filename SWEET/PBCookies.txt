

PB2

1 cup unsalted butter
1 cup crunchy peanut butter

1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed dark brown sugar

5 egg yolks
 
1 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup bread flour

1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

2 teaspoons vanilla

Step 0:
brown butter (1 cup/2 sticks) and cool. Replace water to bring up to volume


Step 1
Cream butter, peanut butter (1 cup), sugars (1 cup white, 1 cup dark brown), vanilla (2 teaspoons) together in a bowl;
beat in eggs yoks (5 egg yolks).

Step 2
In a separate bowl, sift flour (1 cup all purpose/1 cup bread), baking powder(1 1/2 teaspons), baking soda (1 teaspon), and salt (1/2 teaspoon); stir into butter mixture.

Step 3:
Put dough in refrigerator for 1 hour.

Step 4
Roll dough into 1 inch balls (65g) and put on baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F oven for about 15-20 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.
